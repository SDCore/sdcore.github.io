Current Project(s)
myHub
myHub is an in-progress project that focuses on giving specific information in one place for the user to use. At the moment, not much is there. Weather is pulled from the DarkSky API, and directions are using Google Maps Directions API and Google Maps Geocoding API. In the future, I hope to have more information, such as RSS Feeds, Calender Events from a users Google Account, and more!
myInvoice
myInvoice is part of a similar group of apps, much like myHub, and it serves as my own personal invoice system. It's meant to take on a modern, material-type invoice system. This was made purely because I wanted more of an internal system rather than use someone elses.
